## 7. GameTime.ai Relationship

*This section governs how the founders relate to GameTime as a third party.*

- GameTime is currently acting as **distribution partner / sales channel**
- GameTime's role, equity stake (if any), and revenue share must be formalized
  in a separate **Services & License Agreement** before HRS signs
- Key terms to negotiate with GameTime:
  - Do they want equity? At what valuation?
  - What % do they take per tenant they bring?
  - Who owns the client relationship — GameTime or the founders?
  - What happens if GameTime exits or pivots?

**Recommended position:** Founders retain IP. GameTime gets 15–25% of revenue
from clients they source, in exchange for distribution. No equity until a
proper fundraising round with a clear valuation.
